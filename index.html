About Me

Hi! I've been going by my Afghan name, Borhan, my whole life, but my legal name is Brian. I know, it's confusing.

Anyways, welcome to my website! I'm currently in my junior year studying computer science at the University of California, Berkeley. I have strong experience in web development for iOS apps working with JavaScript, JSX, and React Native as well as web application development using JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and Boostrap to integrating data from various API's. My personal interests include investing, watching professional soccer and the NFL, fantasy sports, traveling, and listening to dance, pop, techno, hiphop, and country music. I'm an Afghan-American born in Hayward, raised in San Diego. I'm on track to graduate in the spring of 2021. Hope you enjoy my website!
